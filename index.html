How it Works

Local AI Translate uses a large language model (LLM) that runs entirely on your iPhone. All translation processing happens locally using Apple's Neural Engine and the open-source llama.cpp inference engine.

  • No internet connection required for translation.
  • Your text is never sent to any server or third-party service.
  • The AI model is downloaded once and stored on your device.
